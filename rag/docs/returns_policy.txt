TITLE: OmniTech Returns and Refunds Policy
SOURCE: returns_policy.pdf

Requesting a refund
To request a refund, sign in to your OmniTech account, open Orders, select the
order, and choose "Request refund". Approved refunds are returned to your
original payment method within 5-7 business days. You never need to share your
full credit card number to receive a refund; OmniTech already has your order on
file.

Return window
Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Some categories, such as
opened software, are final sale.

Contacting support
For help with a refund, use the in-app support chat or email
support@omnitech.example.
