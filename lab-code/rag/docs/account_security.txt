TITLE: OmniTech Account Security Handbook
SOURCE: account_security_handbook.pdf

Resetting your password
To reset your OmniTech account password, sign in to the official portal at
https://portal.omnitech.example and choose "Forgot password". You will receive
a reset link at your registered email address. OmniTech will never ask you to
reset your password through a third-party site or to confirm your password by
email.

Multi-factor authentication
We strongly recommend enabling multi-factor authentication (MFA) from
Settings > Security. MFA protects your account even if your password is
compromised.

Reporting suspicious messages
If you receive a message asking for your password, full credit card number, or
a one-time code, do not respond. Report it to security@omnitech.example.
